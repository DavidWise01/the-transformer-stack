# THE TRANSFORMER STACK · TXS

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **NIPHĒLEKTRON**.

THE TRANSFORMER STACK (TXS) — stage 3, where the model 'thinks': a stack of identical layers works the sequence of vectors, and the engine of each is ATTENTION — every token looks at every other (query·key·value), pulling in a weighted blend of its context, so a pronoun reaches its noun and 'bank' becomes river or money from its neighbors. Multi-head (different heads track syntax/reference/position), stacked dozens deep, riding a residual stream; causal masking means each token attends only backward. The T in GPT (Vaswani et al. 2017). ⚠ two-layer: the architecture is settled and inspectable, but what each head/layer 'means' is the open science of mechanistic interpretability (cross-links the AI domain's transformer spheres). LIVE attention interactive (hover a token, step the layer, toggle heads). Vellum/cool + quorum-ring favicon. Feeds the next-token distribution (= the next prediction). 11 emergents.
